Assistive Menu Tool adds a draggable floating button that stays on top of every app. Tap it to open a menu with quick system actions, inspired by the iOS AssistiveTouch feature.

Features:
- Draggable button that snaps to the nearest edge when released
- Animated expandable menu
- System actions: Home, Back, Recents, Lock Screen, Volume Up/Down, Screenshot, Notifications, Quick Settings
- Configurable opacity and button size

Requires two special permissions granted manually:
1. Display over other apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW)
2. Accessibility Service (BIND_ACCESSIBILITY_SERVICE)

No internet access. No analytics. No ads. Fully open source.
